📖 Student Management Guide

How to Edit a Student Profile

  1. Click on a student from the list on the left
  2. The student's information will load in the edit form
  3. Make any changes you need to the following fields:
    • First Name, Last Name: Student's name
    • Email: Must be unique
    • Username: Must be unique
    • Parent Phone: Guardian contact number
    • Class/Grade: JSS1-JSS3 or SSS1-SSS3
    • Department: Only for SSS students (SCIENCE, ART, COMMERCIAL)
    • Password: Leave blank to keep current password
    • Payment Status: Unpaid, Partially Paid, or Fully Paid
  4. Click "Save Changes" to update the profile

⚠️ Important Notes

  • Username and Email must be unique across all students
  • When changing a student from JSS to SSS, you must select a department
  • When changing a student from SSS to JSS, the department is cleared
  • Subjects are automatically assigned based on the department selected
  • Passwords are hashed and never displayed for security
  • Leave password field blank if you don't want to change it

🚨 Deleting Students

Click the "Delete Student" button to permanently remove a student. This action cannot be undone and will also remove all their records (fees, results, etc.).

� Resetting Student Passwords

If a student forgets their password, click the "Reset Password" button. A temporary password will be generated and sent to their email address. They can use this temporary password to log in and then change it to something they'll remember.

💡 Note: The temporary password is sent via email. Make sure the student's email is correct.
